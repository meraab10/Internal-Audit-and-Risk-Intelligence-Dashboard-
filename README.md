# Audit, Compliance & Forensic Intelligence Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project was developed as part of the **May Power BI Challenge by Databuzz**.

The dashboard is designed to transform complex audit, compliance, internal control, and forensic data into clear, decision-focused business intelligence.

Rather than displaying isolated reports, this solution connects risk, compliance, control effectiveness, and fraud indicators into one executive reporting experience.

---

## Project Objective

Organizations often struggle with:

* Disconnected audit and compliance reporting
* Limited visibility into operational and regulatory risk
* Weak control monitoring
* Difficulty identifying suspicious financial patterns

This dashboard addresses those challenges by providing an integrated intelligence platform for decision-makers.

---

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Audit Risk Overview

Provides a high-level view of audit performance and organizational risk.

**Key insights:**

* Total and open findings
* Risk score analysis
* Critical findings
* Departmental risk concentration
* Lead auditor performance
* Risk trend analysis

---

### 2. Compliance Intelligence

Focuses on regulatory compliance and filing performance.

**Key insights:**

* Filing status distribution
* Compliance rate
* Late vs on-time filings
* Delay analysis by filing type
* Department compliance exposure
* Penalty exposure heatmap

---

### 3. Control Intelligence

Evaluates internal controls and operational effectiveness.

**Key insights:**

* Controls tested
* Effective vs ineffective controls
* Material weaknesses
* Deficiency analysis
* Audit engagement performance
* Control effectiveness monitoring

---

### 4. Forensic Intelligence

Uses fraud analytics and statistical techniques to detect suspicious patterns.

**Key insights:**

* Benford analysis
* Fraud risk scatter analysis
* High-risk supplier identification
* Suspicious amount trends
* Fraud exposure heatmap
* Anomaly detection across financial years
